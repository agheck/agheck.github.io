Autonomous Maze-Navigating Robot

I developed a robot capable of navigating through a maze and detecting treasures. The purpose of this robot is not to escape the maze; its sole purpose is to find the treasures hidden in the maze.

Navigation

The robot uses depth first search (DFS) to navigate through the maze. It detects walls through ultrasonic sensors pointed at the front, left, and right side of the robot.

Treasure Detection

The treasures are rapidly blinking IR LEDs. The robot needs to detect that these treasures are present, then send their frequency back to the base station.

The robot detects the lights and their frequencies with phototransistors. It sends the measured frequency back to the base station via RF communication.
